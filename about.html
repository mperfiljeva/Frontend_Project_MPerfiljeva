About Me

My Story

Over the years I’ve been honored to work with great people in GE, Amazon and Blizzard Entertainment, where I designed and developed learning products for measurable performance improvement. With the gradual shift towards technology-assisted learning and my growing focus on e-learning, rather than classroom training, I started to become more and more interested not only in instructional, but also in user experience design. My experience in thorough needs analysis and result-oriented design translated perfectly into UX and Product design and as a result, I’m currently managing a medical learning product for a young startup in Berlin.

When I’m not learning or working, I host pen-and-paper roleplaying sessions, play board games and make desserts worth dying for.
